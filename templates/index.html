Our vision

In our college, most of us go on journeys only with a select group of friends. This leads to journeys being heavily dependent on everyone's schedule to the point that due to the unavailability of even one person many journeys get canceled before they even start. Also, this culture stops us from making new friends. We hope to resolve both these issues so that you can meet new incredible people and go to awesome places.
